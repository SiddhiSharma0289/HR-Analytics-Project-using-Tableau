# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

![HR Analytics Dashboard](HR%20Analytics%20Dashboard.png)

## 📌 Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and salary distribution. The dashboard enables HR professionals and business leaders to identify workforce trends, understand factors contributing to attrition, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall employee attrition.
- Analyze attrition across departments and education fields.
- Understand workforce demographics.
- Evaluate job satisfaction across different job roles.
- Identify attrition patterns by salary and gender.
- Build an interactive dashboard for HR reporting.

## 💡 Key Insights

- Sales department records the highest employee attrition.
- Employees aged between **30–36 years** make up the largest workforce segment.
- Most attrition occurs within the **Up to 5K salary slab**.
- Male employees show a higher attrition count than female employees.
- Life Sciences has the highest attrition among all education fields.
- Job satisfaction levels vary significantly across different job roles.
